## How to Use This Template

1. **Copy the base template** (everything above "Progress Tracking")
2. **Choose your Progress Tracking section** (based on Priority #1)
3. **Paste them together** in your Obsidian Templates folder
4. **Save as:** `Daily Journal.md` (or whatever name you prefer)
5. **Use it daily** with Obsidian's template insertion

**Tip:** Start simple. You can always add more sections later. Better to track 5 things consistently than 20 things sporadically.
